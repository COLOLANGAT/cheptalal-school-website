Frequently Asked Questions

What are the selection criteria?

Selection is based on academic performance, co-curricular activities, and aptitude. We seek well-rounded students who can contribute positively to the school community.

Are there scholarships available?

Yes, we offer scholarships and bursaries to deserving students based on merit and need. Interested applicants should inquire during the application process.

Can I apply for mid-year admission?

Mid-year admissions are considered on a case-by-case basis subject to availability of spaces and meeting admission requirements.

What documents do I need to bring on reporting day?

Original birth certificate, admission letter, medical report, immunization certificate, and school fees receipt are required.
