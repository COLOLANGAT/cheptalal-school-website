Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops critical thinking, creativity, and character in young women, preparing them to be leaders and contributors to society.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence in girls' education, producing well-rounded individuals who are academically proficient, morally upright, and socially responsible.

Our Values

Our History

1995

School Established

CHEPTALAL GIRLS SECONDARY SCHOOL was founded with a vision to provide quality education to both girlchild and boychild in the region and was leter converted to girl school in the year 2014.

2005

Expansion Phase

The school expanded its facilities and increased its student population through improved infrastructure and good performance across the region.

2013

ICT Integration

Introduction of modern technology and digital learning resources across all departments. Equiped computer laboratory with internet connectivityand upto date computers was build. In that year, computer studies was enrolled as compulsory subject to all students.

Present

Continuing Excellence

Currently recognized as the leading girls secondary schools in the region with consistent academic excellence.The school is currently bulding a technological resource center equiped with computers that offer educational resources to students and teachers to enhance learning and teaching experiences.
